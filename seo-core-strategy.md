# SEO Core Strategy – All Search Engines

This document defines the **core SEO architecture** for getting your site noticed across all major search engines (Google, Bing, Yandex, Baidu, DuckDuckGo, etc.), while staying ready for your future instructions.

## 1. Core Files Driving SEO

These are the **top files** that power SEO for your site:

- `robots.txt` – Crawl instructions for all search engines  
- `sitemap.xml` – XML sitemap auto‑generated by `one-file-everything.js`  
- `index.html` – Main landing page with strong semantic structure  
- `hotels-in-[city].html` – City‑level hotel pages with unique, enhanced descriptions  
- `all-inclusive-[city].html` – All‑inclusive resort pages optimized for long‑tail queries  
- `reserve-hotel-[city].html` – Booking‑intent pages with affiliate‑link‑focused micro‑copy  
- `where-to-stay-[city].html` – Itinerary‑style pages with neighborhood‑based content  
- `brands-[brand].html` – Brand‑hub pages targeting “Sheraton in [city]”, “Hilton in [city]”, etc.  
- `event-super-bowl-[city].html` – Event‑hotel pages for high‑traffic sporting events  

All of these use:

- Schema.org `Hotel` JSON‑LD  
- Booking.com affiliate links (`aid=1858279`)  
- Skyscanner affiliate links (`offer_id=29465&aff_id=21885`)

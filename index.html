Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the lowest hotel rate? +
Enter your destination and travel dates into the search box above. Our system instantly compares live rates across 2.5 million+ properties and surfaces the lowest available price, including exclusive member-only discounts you won't find anywhere else.
Is there really no hidden fee? +
Correct. The price you see is the price you pay. All taxes and fees are shown upfront before you confirm. There are no booking fees or surprise charges added at checkout.
What is the price-match guarantee? +
If you find the same room at the same property for a lower price on any other site within 24 hours of booking, we'll refund the difference. You're guaranteed to get the best available rate.
Can I cancel or change my reservation? +
Many properties offer free cancellation up to 24–48 hours before check-in. Filter for "free cancellation only" in the search to see only those properties. Non-refundable rates are clearly marked and typically offer deeper discounts.
Is my payment information secure? +
Yes. All transactions use 256-bit SSL encryption — the same standard used by major banks. Your card details are never stored on our servers and are processed through a fully PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway.
Do I need to create an account to book? +
No account is required. Simply search, choose your property, and complete your booking. Creating a free account does unlock Genius member prices (up to 20% extra off) and lets you manage all reservations in one place.
